Hello there!

I am Kyel Linn and I am certified UI designer.
Before start diving too deep, let me talk about my background a bit. I have been working in graphic design industry in agency and as freelancer for 4 years. I have been a successful designer in my past years. Even after finding right design solution for my clients, some are not quite effective as I thought. With the above and beyond mindset, I find a way to improve myself then I found user experience design. It was the love at first sight. It really fascinating to learn human psychology and how human unconscious mind work. I have finished 3 case study thanks to Google UX design Certificate program and have some grip about usability and design theory thanks to User Interface Design Specialization Certificate program offer from University of Minnesota and ready to take on real-world challenge head on.
